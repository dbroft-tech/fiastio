Our Story

A journey of passion, tradition, and the love of bringing people together through exceptional food.

From Family Kitchen to Your Table

Feastio was born from a simple dream: to share the authentic flavors of Mediterranean home cooking with the world. Our founder, Chef Maria Kostas, grew up in a small village on the coast of Greece, where every meal was a celebration of fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes.

In 2015, Maria brought her family's recipes to San Francisco, determined to create a space where guests could experience the warmth and joy of a Mediterranean family gathering. What started as a humble corner restaurant has grown into a beloved destination for food lovers seeking authentic, healthful cuisine.

Today, Feastio remains true to its roots. We source the finest ingredients, many imported directly from the Mediterranean, and prepare each dish with the same love and care that Maria learned in her grandmother's kitchen.
